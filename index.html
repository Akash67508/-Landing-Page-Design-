You Started!

Frontend development is the art of crafting interactive and visually appealing user interfaces for websites and web applications. It involves using a combination of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to create layouts, style elements, and add functionality that users can interact with directly in their browsers. Frontend developers work closely with designers to bring their visions to life and ensure a seamless user experience. They must also prioritize responsiveness and accessibility, making sure that their creations work well across different devices and are usable by people with disabilities. In an ever-evolving digital landscape, frontend development continues to push boundaries, incorporating new technologies and design trends to enhance user engagement.
